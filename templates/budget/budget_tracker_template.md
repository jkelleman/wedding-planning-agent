## ğŸ’¡ Budget Tips & Notes

### Setting Your Budget
- **Be realistic** - Research average costs in your area
- **Prioritize** - What matters most to you?
- **Build in contingency** - Add 10-15% buffer for unexpected costs
- **Consider hidden costs** - Tips, taxes, overtime fees
- **Track from day one** - Don't wait to start budgeting

### Sticking to Your Budget
- **Get multiple quotes** - Compare at least 3 vendors per category
- **Read contracts carefully** - Watch for hidden fees
- **Ask about payment plans** - Many vendors offer installments
- **Consider off-season/weekday** - Can save 20-40%
- **DIY strategically** - Save money where it makes sense for you
- **Don't forget tips** - Plan for 15-20% gratuity for most services

### When to Splurge
- Photography (you have these forever)
- Food (guests will remember)
- Items that reflect your personality
- Things that matter most to YOU

### Where to Save
- Flowers (in-season blooms, greenery, DIY)
- Invitations (digital or DIY)
- Favors (skip them or DIY)
- Decor (borrow, DIY, or rent)
- Off-peak dates/times

### Hidden Costs to Remember
- [ ] Gratuities (15-20% for most vendors)
- [ ] Overtime fees (if event runs long)
- [ ] Delivery/setup fees
- [ ] Tax (7-10% in most areas)
- [ ] Marriage license
- [ ] Alterations
- [ ] Postage
- [ ] Vendor meals
- [ ] Cake cutting fees (if venue charges)
- [ ] Corkage fees (if bringing own alcohol)
