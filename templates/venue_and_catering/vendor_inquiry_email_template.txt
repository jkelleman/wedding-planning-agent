1. **Be specific with numbers** - Venues appreciate detailed inquiries

3. **Ask about ALL fees upfront** - Gratuity, admin, tax, cake cutting, etc.

6. **Be honest about budget** - Saves everyone time

8. **Clarify what's included** - Tables, chairs, linens, china, etc.
9. **Understand the timeline** - Setup, event, breakdown
10. **Get tasting info** - Most venues offer tastings for events
