##  Tips for Using This Template

1. **Get everything in writing** - Ask venue for itemized quote
2. **Don't forget fees** - Gratuity, admin fees, and taxes can add 30-40% to subtotal
3. **Plan for dietary restrictions** - Survey guests early
4. **Account for non-drinkers** - Don't overbuy alcohol
5. **Ask about minimums** - Room minimums often include food + beverage combined
6. **Consider timing** - Lunch is often cheaper than dinner
7. **Request leftovers** - Many venues will box up remaining food
8. **Compare apples to apples** - Some venues include more in base price
9. **Read the fine print** - Understand cancellation and payment terms
10. **Visit in person** - Photos don't capture everything
