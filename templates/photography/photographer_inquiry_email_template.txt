## üí° Email Tips for Contacting Photographers

### Do's:
‚úÖ **Be specific about your date and location**  
‚úÖ **Mention what you love about their work**

### Don'ts:
‚ùå Don't send vague inquiries ("How much for wedding photos?")  
‚ùå Don't hide your budget - they need to know if it's realistic  
‚ùå Don't copy-paste generic emails - personalize each one  
‚ùå Don't contact 50 photographers at once - start with top 5-10  
‚ùå Don't be pushy about quick responses - give 3-5 business days  
‚ùå Don't ask for free work or discounts in first email  
‚ùå Don't forget to follow up if no response after 1 week  

### What Photographers Want to Know:
1. **Your date** - Are they even available?

3. **Your budget** - Can they work with it?
4. **Hours needed** - What coverage do you want?

6. **Event type** - Elopement, traditional, destination?
