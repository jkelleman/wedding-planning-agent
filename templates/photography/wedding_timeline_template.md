## ‚è±Ô∏è Timing Tips & Recommendations

### Allow Extra Time For:
- **Travel between locations:** Add 15-30 min buffer
- **Large family groups:** 2-3 min per combination
- **Outfit changes:** 15-30 min
- **Unexpected delays:** Build in 15 min cushion

### Optimal Photography Times:
- **Golden hour:** 1 hour before sunset (warm, flattering light)
- **Blue hour:** Just after sunset (romantic, moody)
- **Harsh midday sun:** Avoid 11am-2pm if possible, or shoot in shade
- **Indoor/covered locations:** Flexible timing

### Recommended Coverage Duration by Event Type:
- **Elopement (ceremony only):** 1-2 hours
- **Elopement (ceremony + portraits):** 2-3 hours
- **Intimate wedding (20-50 guests):** 4-6 hours
- **Traditional wedding (50-150 guests):** 8-10 hours
- **Large wedding (150+ guests):** 10-12 hours

##  Photography Timeline Tips

### Before Creating Your Timeline:
1. **Know your priorities** - What moments matter most?
2. **Consider the light** - Plan outdoor shots during golden hour
3. **Factor in travel time** - Between ceremony/reception/photo locations
4. **Consult your photographer** - They know how long things actually take
5. **Be realistic** - Don't cram too much into too little time

### Creating the Timeline:
1. **Start with fixed times** - Ceremony, sunset, venue access
2. **Work backwards** - When do you need to be ready?
3. **Build in buffer time** - Things always take longer than expected
4. **Prioritize family photos** - These can be time-consuming
5. **Plan for golden hour** - If you want those warm, glowing photos

### Sharing with Your Photographer:
- Send timeline 2-4 weeks before wedding
- Include addresses and parking for all locations
- Note any special moments or traditions
- Share family photo list with names
- Designate point person for wrangling family members
- Be open to their suggestions for timing adjustments

### Day-of Execution:
- Stick to timeline as much as possible
- Empower point person to keep things moving
- Trust your photographer's direction during portraits
- Don't stress if running 10-15 min behind
- Focus on moments, not just photos
