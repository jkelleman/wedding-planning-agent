##  How to Use These Files

### Phase 1: Research & Selection (8-12 weeks before)
1. Review `photographer_comparison.md` to identify top 3-5 photographers
2. Check `photographer_contacts.md` for their contact info
3. Customize and send `photographer_inquiry_email.txt`
4. Compare responses, review portfolios
5. Schedule consultations with top 2-3
6. Select photographer and book!

### Phase 2: Planning & Coordination (4-8 weeks before)
1. Open `wedding_day_timeline_and_logistics.md`
2. Share timeline with photographer
3. Review shot list together
4. Discuss location logistics and permissions
5. Confirm backup plans for weather
6. Go through planning checklist

### Phase 3: Final Details (1-2 weeks before)
1. Confirm photographer arrival time
2. Send final guest count
3. Verify all locations and times
4. Review family grouping list
5. Confirm contact info for day-of
