# Venue & Catering Planning

This folder contains all documents related to your venue selection, catering, menu planning, and guest communications.

## ðŸ“„ Files in This Folder

### `venue_cost_analysis.md`
**Purpose:** Complete cost breakdown for your venue/restaurant  
**Includes:** Menu selections, beverage packages, fees, taxes, total cost  
**When to use:** When comparing venues, tracking budget, finalizing menu

### `vendor_inquiry_email.txt`
**Purpose:** Email to send to venue with questions and menu selections  
**Format:** Plain text - ready to copy/paste  
**When to use:** Initial vendor outreach, asking questions, confirming availability

### `vendor_inquiry_email_formatted.txt`
**Purpose:** Same email with formatting for Gmail/Outlook  
**Format:** Rich text with section headers  
**When to use:** If you want a more polished email appearance

### `guest_invitation_details.txt`
**Purpose:** Information to share with guests about the event  
**Includes:** Location, timing, menu, dietary accommodations, parking  
**When to use:** Send to guests 4-6 weeks before, include in invitation suite

### `guest_invitation_details_formatted.txt`
**Purpose:** Same content formatted for Word/email with rich styling  
**When to use:** Creating formal invitation enclosures or nice emails
