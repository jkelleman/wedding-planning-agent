##  Music Delivery Options

### Option 1: DIY Playlist (Most Affordable)
- **Platform:** Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube Music
- **Cost:** Free (with ads) or $10-15/month premium
- **Setup:** Create playlists for each section, designate someone to manage transitions
- **Pros:** Full control, personal song selection, very affordable
- **Cons:** Need someone to manage it, potential for technical issues

### Option 2: The Banks' Sound System
- **Ask The Banks:** Do they have a house sound system?
- **Can you:** Connect your phone/laptop via Bluetooth or aux cable?
- **Do they provide:** Basic background music?

### Option 3: Hire a DJ (Partial Service)
- **Service:** DJ for ceremony + reception (not full wedding DJ)
- **Cost:** $500-$1,500 for 4-5 hours (smaller event pricing)
- **Pros:** Professional sound, smooth transitions, no technical stress
- **Cons:** Additional cost

### Option 4: Live Musician (Special Touch)
- **Options:** 
  - Solo guitarist/pianist for ceremony ($300-600)
  - Jazz duo for cocktail hour ($400-800)
- **Pros:** Elegant, live music creates special ambiance
- **Cons:** Higher cost, need to coordinate with venue
