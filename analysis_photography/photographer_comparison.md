## üìã What to Ask Photographers

### Initial Inquiry Questions:
1. Are you available on [your date]?
2. What's your pricing for a 3-hour event covering 3 locations (hotel, BPL, restaurant)?
3. Do you have experience shooting at the Boston Public Library?
4. Have you photographed at The Banks or similar Boston restaurants?
5. What's included in your packages?
   - Number of edited photos delivered
   - Turnaround time
   - Digital rights
   - Online gallery
   - Print rights
6. Can we see full galleries from similar multi-location intimate events?
7. What's your photography style? (Documentary, posed, mix)
8. Do you charge travel fees between locations in the same area?
9. Do you offer payment plans?
10. What's your backup plan if you're unable to shoot?
11. Can we schedule a call or meeting to discuss?

### Follow-Up Questions:
12. Do you have liability insurance? (Required by BPL for commercial photography)
13. What's your cancellation/postponement policy?
14. Will you be the photographer on the day, or an associate?
15. How many photos can we expect from 3 hours across 3 locations?
16. Do you offer add-ons (engagement session, prints, albums)?
17. What's your editing timeline?
18. Are you comfortable navigating between three locations in Back Bay?
19. Do you need any special permits for BPL photography?
20. What's your approach to weather contingencies?

## üó∫Ô∏è Location Details & Logistics

### The Lenox Hotel
- **Address:** 61 Exeter Street, Boston, MA
- **Distance to BPL:** 0.3 miles (5-7 min walk)
- **Best for:** Classic, elegant hotel backdrop
- **Photography spots:** Lobby, rooms (if booking), exterior
- **Permission needed:** Check with hotel for public space photography
- **Backup plan:** If not staying at Lenox, photographer can meet at BPL

### Boston Public Library (Copley Square)
- **Address:** 700 Boylston Street, Boston, MA
- **Distance BPL to The Banks:** 0.4 miles (8 min walk / 5 min drive)
- **Best for:** Iconic Boston architecture, grand interiors
- **Photography spots:**
  - **Exterior:** Main facade, courtyard, fountain
  - **Interior:** Grand staircase, Bates Hall (reading room), arched corridors
- **Permission needed:** BPL allows photography in public areas
  - Large commercial shoots require permit
  - Small family portraits generally OK (check with librarian)
  - Some restrictions in special collections areas
- **Best time:** Late afternoon (4:30-5:15 PM) = beautiful light
- **Backup plan:** If BPL is crowded/closed, nearby Copley Square or Trinity Church exterior

### The Banks Seafood and Steak
- **Address:** 406 Stuart Street, Boston, MA
- **Private room:** Fireplace Room
- **Best for:** Candid reception moments, intimate dinner
- **Photography spots:** Private dining room, table settings, candids
- **Permission:** Private room = full access for photography

### Travel Logistics
- **Lenox ‚Üí BPL:** 5-7 min walk
- **BPL ‚Üí The Banks:** 8 min walk OR quick drive/Uber
- **All locations within 0.5 mile radius** = very manageable
- **Photographer should plan for:**
  - 5-10 min transition time between locations
  - Weather backup (indoor options at each)
  - Equipment for multiple settings
